    About Us

    The Centre of Excellence for Assistive Technology and Rehabilitant Engineering Solutions (PSG CARES) was established in the year 2019 partly under DST –SEED division funding, with the vision of promoting it as a CoE of world class standard in the field of smart healthcare. The center was inagurated by Dr Konga Gopikrishna , Scientist, Department of Science and Technology on 9th August 2019.

    Our Vision

    To become a Centre of Excellence of world class standard in the field of Assistive Technology and Rehabilitation Engineering to enhance the Quality of Life (QoL) of Geriatric and Specially Challenged population through Innovative Technology Interventions

    Our Mission

    To develop innovative and cost effective solutions for addressing the frail population by leveraging the emerging technological advancements with Interdisciplinary approach.

    To partner with government, industry and non-profit organizations to facilitate commercialization and mass production of the assistive technology interventions developed in the center.

    To promote entrepreneurship potential in the areas of Wearable Assistive Technology and Rehabilitation Engineering and become a Certified Testing Centre for Smart Wearable Biomedical Devices.

    Objectives

    • To design and develop innovative and cost effective smart health care solutions by leveraging the advancements in medical IoT, Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics etc.

    • To conduct clinical and field validation trials with the help of health care professionals

    • To publish Research Findings to disseminate state-of-art knowledge in the fields of Assistive Technology, Smart Wearable Devices and Modern Rehabilitation Engineering

    • To work out sustainable business models to commercialize the technologies so as to reach out to the masses

    Target Beneficiaries

    Geriatric patients with weak / impaired gait

    Post operative patients of Spine, Hip, Knee, and other orthopedic conditions

    Patients suffering from Parkinson, Stroke, Dementia, and other neurological disorders

    Patients who are on anti-coagulation drugs

    Patients with Cardio Vascular Diseases
